# ADOBO
Ingredients
Meat
• 1 3-pound slab Pork belly, boneless and skin on
Produce
• 4 Bay leaves
• 5 cloves Garlic
• 1 Onion, medium
Condiments
• 2 tbsp Oyster sauce
• 1/2 cup Soy sauce, light
Baking & Spices
• 1 tsp Brown sugar
• 1 tbsp Peppercorns, whole black
• 1 Salt
Oils & Vinegars
• 1/4 cup Vinegar
Liquids
• 1 cup Water
